National Flag

The National Flag is a horizontal tricolourof India saffron(kesari)at the top.white in the middle and India green at the bottom in equal proportion.

National Anthem

The song Jana-Gana-Mana,composed originally in bangala by Rabindranath Tagore,was adopted in its Hindi version by the constituent Assembly as the National Anthem of India on janurary24,1950.

National song

The song Vande Mataram,composed in sanskrit by Bankimchandra Chaterjji,was asource of inspiriation to the people in their struggle towards freedom It has an equal status as Jana-Gana-Mana.

National Bird

the Indian peacock,Pavo cristatus,the National bird of India,is a colourful,swan-sized Bird,with a fan-shaped crest of feathers,a white patch under the eye and a long,slender neck.

National Animal

The national animal of India is the Tiger (Panthera tigris), specifically the Royal Bengal Tiger. its known for its thick yellow coat of fur with dark stripes and Symbolizes India's wildlife wealth, power, and ecological balance
